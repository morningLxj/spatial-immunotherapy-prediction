# Reporting and Reviewer Boundaries

This study was revised using a conservative reporting stance aligned with observational and prediction-model reporting principles.

## What the Study Supports

- C1Q/SPP1 can be traced across public transcriptomic, spatial, and immune-context resources.
- The model behavior is retrospective and exploratory.
- MR results prioritize candidate genes for follow-up.
- Spatial results support context-level association.

## What the Study Does Not Support

- No clinical implementation claim.
- No treatment-selection claim.
- No therapeutic target validation.
- No definitive causal mechanism.
- No direct cell-cell signaling proof.
- No prospective clinical validation.

## Reviewer-Sensitive Issues Addressed

- TCGA discovery sample count is distinguished from QC-filtered analytic count.
- GSE31210 total accession-level samples are distinguished from complete-follow-up samples.
- GSE91061 is not treated as NSCLC-specific validation.
- CD138 is treated as plasma-cell context, not a macrophage marker.
- Counterfactual C1QA attenuation is described as scoring sensitivity, not perturbation.
- External results are described as traceability or exploratory association, not definitive validation.
